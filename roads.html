Where Did They Race?

These japanese street racers raced on mountain roads, hence the name “touge”. These
mountain roads are most known for for how bendy they are. Since they are mountain
roads, they have an uphill and a downhill. People raced on both the uphill and the
downhill, but most people agree that racing the downhill takes more skill considering
the downwards slope can even make a slow car go fast.
