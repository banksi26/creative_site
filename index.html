What Is Touge Racing?

Touge racing is a type of racing where you don’t do much racing, but instead just go out and have fun
messing around in cars. The word “touge” is a japanese word which translates to “mountain pass”. Touge
racing is basically just street racing, except on a twisty mountain road.

Is It Legal?

No, touge racing is not legal, it’s still just street racing. But people still did it even though it isn’t legal.
The surprising thing is that most people, including cops, didn’t care if people were street racing. The
racers weren’t actually bothering anyone so people just let them continue having fun.
